Guta Challenge

Challenge 1:

Translate:
  • How do you like your soup?
  • I like it a lot. It is warm and tasty.

Dictionary:
  • vi = how
  • paju = like
  • zupa = soup
  • guto = tasty
  • lo sonu = it is
  • filo = much
Maj:
  • Vi te paju toa zupa?
  • Lo paju filo. Lo sonu xado cy guto.

Challenge 2:

Translate:
  • My soup is very pungent. I do not like it!
Dictionary:
  • zupa = soup
  • mui = very
  • akro = pungent
  • no paju=pa = not liking
Maj:
  • Mi zupa sonu mui akro; ea no lo paju-pa!

Challenge 3:

Translate:
  • My soup is more salty than usual. I hate it!
Dictionary:
  • plu = more
  • salo = salty
  • do = than
  • iujo = usual
  • xasu = hate
Maj:
  • Mi zupa sonu plu salo do iujo. Ea lo xasu!

Challenge 4:

Translate:
  • What do you wish to have for desert?
Dictionary
  • ke = what
  • fo = for
  • doru = wish
  • sevu = serve
  • doca = dessert
Maj:
  • Ke doru-toa sh sevu fo doca?

Challenge 5:

Translate:
  • I wish to take a slice of fruit pie.
Dictionary:
  • sh lotu = to take
  • al = of
  • ut = with
  • poma = fruit
  • tarta = pie
  • feta= slice
    • Maj:
    • Ea doru sh lotu una feta al tarta ku poma.

    Challenge 6:

    Translate:
    • How was your pie?
    • My pie was sweet and soft.
    Dictionary:
  • vi = how
  • sonu-pa = it was
  • ci = and
  • dulo = soft

    • Maj:
    • Vi sonu-pa toa tarta?
    • Mia tarta sonu-pa duco ci dulo.

    Challenge 7:

    Translate:
    • How often do you eat in a day?
    • How often do you eat in a day?
    Dictionary:
  • ofh = often
  • ogi = each
  • iujo = usually
  • ore = times
  • bo = but
  • djo = only
    • Maj:
    • Vi ofh toa moku oni djya?
    • Iujo du-ore da algo-tepa djo un-ora.
