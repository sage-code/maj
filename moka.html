Moka Challenge

Challenge 1:

Translate: When is the time to eat?
Dictionary:
  • ut = when
  • sonu = is
  • tepa = time
  • moku = to eat
Maj: Ut sonu el tepa sh moku?

Challenge 2:

Translate:
  • If one is hungry, he must eat something.
  • There are people who suffer from hunger.
Dictionary:
  • si = if
  • su = he
  • ki = who
  • una = one
  • xaga = hunger
  • dovu = must
  • sh moku = to eat
  • algo = something
  • aka = there
  • Xete = people
Maj:
  • Si un sonu hago, su dovu sh moku algo.
  • Aka sonu Xete ki sofu de xaga.

Challenge 3:

Translate:
  • Which is your favorite food?
  • My favorite food is omelette with cheese.
Dictionary:
  • uk = which
  • fevo = favorite
  • mia = my
  • fevo = favorite
  • moka = food
  • omla = omelette
  • de = from
  • kesa = cheese
Maj:
  • Uk sonu toa moka fevo?
  • Mia moka fevo sonu la omla de kesa.

Challenge 4:

Translate:
  • Do you like toast with butter?
  • I don-t like it. I prefer to eat without bread.
Dictionary
  • tsie = to you
  • te paju = you like
  • pana = bread
  • fejo = fried
  • ku = with
  • buta = butter
  • og = without
Maj:
  • tsie te paju la pana fejo ku buta?
  • no lo paju; moa favu sh moku og pana.

Challenge 5:

Translate:
  • Do you like boiled eggs?
  • Yes, I like to eat two boiled eggs with mustard for breakfast;
Dictionary:
  • te = to you
  • la ueva = the egg (singular)
  • le ueve = eggs (plural)
  • geko = boiled
  • sefa = mustard
  • deja = breakfast
    • Maj:
    • te paju le ueve geko?
    • xa, ym paju sh moku bie weve geko ku sefa po deja;
