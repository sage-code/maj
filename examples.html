Maj Examples

Work in progress.

Example 1

Translate:
  • The squirrel is in the hollow.
  • The squirrel love nuts.
Dictionary:
  • squirrel = Ardila
  • hollow = korba
Maj:
  • dh Ardila sonu yn korba
  • dh Ardila lomu Nuke.

Example 2

Translate:
  • The dove eats grains.
  • The dove flies far.
Dictionary:
  • dove = Paloma
  • grains = Gryne
Maj:
  • dh Paloma moku Gryne.
  • dh Paloma volu lano.

Example 3

Translate:
  • The cat chases the mouse.
  • The cat hunts the mouse.
  • The mouse hides in the hollow.
Dictionary:
  • cat = Mytsa
  • mouse = Sorina
Maj:
  • dh Mytsa fogu dh Sorina.
  • dh Mytsa jagu dh Sorina.
  • dh Sorina kasu yn korba.

Example 4

Translate:
  • The kids are on the bridge.
  • They look at the ducks.
  • A duck has 3 ducklings.
  • Two ducks fly scared.
Dictionary:
  • duck = Anasa
  • duckling = Boba
  • brika = bridge
Maj:
  • dh Kide sonu pe brika
  • zhi vhzu dh Anase
  • una Anasa xaru rie Bobe
  • bie Anase volu sparo
